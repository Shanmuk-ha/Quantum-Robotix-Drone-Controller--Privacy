Short version: QR Drone Controller does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Overview

Quantum Robotix ("we", "us", or "our") developed the QR Drone Controller app. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of the app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information we collect

We collect no personal information. QR Drone Controller operates entirely on your local device and communicates only with your drone over a local Wi-Fi network. No data leaves your device to our servers or any third party.

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Flight control settings (PID values, angle strength, roll and pitch trim) — stored locally via Android SharedPreferences
  • Recorded video footage — saved directly to your device's gallery

You can delete your locally stored settings at any time by uninstalling the app. You can delete recorded videos from your device gallery at any time.

3. Network communications

QR Drone Controller communicates exclusively with your drone hardware over a local Wi-Fi connection using UDP on your home or local network. No data is transmitted to the internet. The app does not make any connections to remote servers, analytics platforms, or cloud services.

The app requests Wi-Fi and network access permissions solely to detect and establish a connection to your drone's access point.

4. Camera and video recording

If your drone is equipped with a camera, QR Drone Controller can receive and display a live video feed transmitted from your drone over the local Wi-Fi connection. You can optionally record this feed to your device.

Recorded videos are saved directly to your device's local gallery and are never uploaded, shared, or transmitted by the app. The app does not access any other photos or media on your device.

5. Permissions used

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — Required to communicate with your drone over the local Wi-Fi network
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE — Required to detect whether you are connected to Wi-Fi before attempting to pair
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Required to check network connectivity status
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — Required on older Android versions to save recorded video to your gallery

All permissions are used solely for the functionality described above. We do not use any permission for tracking, advertising, or data collection.

6. Third-party services

QR Drone Controller does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or social media SDKs. There are no trackers of any kind in this app.

7. Children's privacy

QR Drone Controller is intended for users aged 18 and above. Operating a drone requires adult supervision and compliance with local aviation regulations. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can address it.

8. Data security

Since QR Drone Controller does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no personal data at risk of a breach on our end. Your locally stored flight settings are protected by your device's own security (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

9. Your rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data held by us for you to access, correct, export, or delete. All data generated by the app (settings and recordings) remains entirely under your control on your own device.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or applicable laws. When we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
